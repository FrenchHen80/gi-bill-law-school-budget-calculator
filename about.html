About This Tool

The GI Bill Law School Budget Calculator is designed to help veterans estimate their monthly finances while attending law school using the Post-9/11 GI Bill.

The Monthly Housing Allowance (MHA) varies by school location, so choosing where to attend law school can significantly impact your cost of living. This tool allows you to compare housing allowance, rent, and other expenses to better understand your financial situation.

Whether you're deciding between schools or planning your budget, this calculator gives you a simple way to visualize how far your benefits will go.
