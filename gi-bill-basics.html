GI Bill Basics

A quick, practical overview of how Post-9/11 GI Bill benefits typically work for law school.

Monthly Housing Allowance (MHA): Usually based on the school’s ZIP code and your rate of pursuit (full-time vs. part-time).

Tuition & Fees: Paid to the school (public in-state generally covered; private depends on the annual cap + Yellow Ribbon if available).

Book Stipend: Typically paid to you, separate from MHA.

Timing: Payments often lag at the start of a term—plan for a buffer.
